NEIL GAIMAN


Neil Gaiman writes books for all ages and has various childrens novels as well as adult themed books turned film. He has been titled with being one of the creators of modern comics. He is listed in the Dictionary of Literary Biography as one of the top ten living post-modern writers and is a prolific creator of multiple works or prose, poetry, film, journalism,comics, song lyrics, and drama. Neil Gaiman's work has been honoured with many awards internationally, including the Newbery and Carnegie Medals. His books and stories have also been honoured with Hugos, Nebulas, the World Fantasy Award, Bram Stoker Awards, Locus Awards, British SF Awards, British Fantasy Awards, Geffens, Mythopoeic Awards, and numerous others.
